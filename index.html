Bangladesh Road Transport Authority (BRTA) has cancelled the registration of Bepari Paribahan bus ((Dhaka Metro Ba 14-5170) involved in Friday's road crash on the Dhaka-Mawa Expressway.

"We have cancelled the registration of the bus as it had been operation without fitness clearance and route permit," BRTA Director (Dhaka division) Mohammad Shahidulla told The Daily Star today.

Six people of two families were killed when the bus rammed into three other vehicles parked at the toll plaza of the expressway in South Keraniganj yesterday morning.
